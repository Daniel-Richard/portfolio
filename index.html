I'm Kel, a user experience and product designer in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am dedicated to creating experiences that enrich the lives of others through human-centered design. I'm passionate about creating products that empower individuals to live healthy lives, both physically and mentally and products that foster communication and community. I believe that great design is created when users are at the heart of the design process. I love technology because of its immediacy and ability to engage so many different types of audiences. I want to create digital products that bring a high level of value into people's lives. In the past, I have designed objects as diverse as wall-covering for a hospital in Qatar while working in a Chelsea loft in NYC and column covers for a casino in Nevada while working at a factory in East Oakland. My diverse experience as a designer and background in manufacturing has given me a deep appreciation for the products that make up our everyday lives. When I'm not designing, you can find me collecting new hobbies like making kimchi, practicing archery, studying the Enneagram or volunteering my time at local women's right organizations.
